Focal Point . The History of Computers
Learning . Before Electricity
  • The human mind
  • The Abacus
  • Pascal's adding and subtracting machine

Electrical Circuits Utilized

  • Atanasoff-Berry-Computer 1937
  • ENIAC, the first large-scale electronic general-purpose digital computer 1946
  • The transistor developed 1947.  The transistor took the place of the vacuum tube.  These devices are on/off switches.  They were natural devices to implement the binary number system which we will discuss on 8 Oct
  • The first computers using transistors were produced introducing the 2nd Generation of computers 1958
  • Xerox invents the first local area network 1975

Microcomputers Introduced

  • MITS introduces the Altair 1975
  • The Apple computer is introduced 1976
  • The IBM PC is introduced 1981
  • The Macintosh is introduced as the first widely available personal computer with a graphical interface

The State of the Industry

  • The IBM PC compatible has about 95% of the current personal computer market share.  The other 5% mainly belongs to Apple's Macintosh line
  • The latest processors run at up to 1.7 Ghz
  • RAM prices are about $20 for 128MB
