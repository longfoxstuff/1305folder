Focal Point . Math - Binary and Hexadecimal
Learning . Binary
  • Base 2
  • Ones and Zeroes
  • Adding
  • Multiplying

Hexadecimal

  • Base 16
  • Digits go from Zero to "F"
  • Converting
  • Nybbles are half-bytes which convert easily into one hex digit

Why?

  • Binary is used inside the machine because of the digital nature of computers
  • Transistors have only two states which indicates binary
  • Hex is a much more compact representation than binary
  • Hex is easily converted to and from binary
